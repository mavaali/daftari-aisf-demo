> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Op MARIGOLD — after-action

Closed Q4 2022. Cairo Antiquarians-adjacent watch operation; intended to identify Egyptian-side document infrastructure. Modest outputs; closed without escalation when the Antiquarians' Cairo presence proved smaller and more intermittent than the working hypothesis assumed.

## Outcomes

- Confirmed the Antiquarians' Cairo footprint is occasional/transit, not standing.
- Identified two small print shops of interest (one still on a low-tempo watch).
- No asset cultivation reached steady state.

## Lessons

1. Negative findings are findings. MARIGOLD's principal output was a reliable "absence" map — operationally valuable for scoping subsequent operations.
2. Don't escalate when the absence is the finding.
