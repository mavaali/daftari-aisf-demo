> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Identity

[POI: Halloran, E.] — male, 64, British national, London-based. Public face: rare-book dealer, Mayfair premises. Private function: principal node of the Antiquarians' London hub — the laundering/storage layer for high-value manuscripts and (per recent reporting, contested) other portable assets.

## Role in Antiquarians

- Sets the consignment intake norms across the London ↔ Geneva axis.
- Personal relationship with [POI: Vasari, B.] going back at least two decades.
- Visits Geneva monthly; visits BA twice yearly (auction season).

Source reliability: **C/3**.

## Product taxonomy (asset classes)

The canonical Antiquarians product mix for Halloran's hub is understood to be:

- **Manuscripts**: vellum, illuminated work, early printed books, sealed correspondence.
- **Works on paper**: drawings, plate engravings, maps, early photography.
- **Sealed lots**: provenance-uncertain bundles handled discreetly.

The question of whether Halloran handles **coins / numismatics** is open. Baseline reporting from [ASSET CARDINAL](../assets/cardinal.md) said no — coins refused on principle. May 2026 reporting from the same source said yes — coins moved last month. Tension [T5](../../tensions/tension-005.md).
