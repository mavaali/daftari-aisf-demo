> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Active sites

### MILK — Tiergarten (north loop)
- Bench leg, north side; sub-bench fixed cavity.
- Chalk-mark site: lamp post at junction, knee-height.
- Drop window: 07:00–09:00; pickup window: 17:00–19:00.
- Currently frozen pending [BLUEBIRD stand-down](../../field-reports/berlin/2026-06-02-bluebird-stand-down.md).

### OAK — Prenzlauer Berg
- Tree-root cavity, eastern aspect of designated oak.
- Chalk-mark site: adjacent bus shelter.
- Drop/pickup windows variable (24-hour usable, low-traffic).
- Status: available, lightly used.

### SPARROW — Kreuzberg
- Cafe-toilet third stall, paper-towel dispenser back-panel.
- Chalk-mark site: street-side window planter.
- Drop window: 11:00–14:00.
- Status: active.

## Discipline

Maximum two operations per site per quarter. Operator must visit each site cold (no purpose) at least monthly to maintain familiarity.
