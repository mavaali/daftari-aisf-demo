> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Pattern library

### Commercial-registry consultation
- **Origin:** Meridian Trade & Records standing cover.
- **Use:** Berlin, Cairo desks predominantly.
- **Strength:** boring, paper-heavy, durable.
- **Limit:** does not extend to social settings.

### Trade-association presence
- **Origin:** legitimate Bureau-funded memberships.
- **Use:** Tokyo (HERON cultivation).
- **Strength:** sustains long-horizon cultivation in business contexts.
- **Limit:** identification risk via attendance records.

### Gallery / auction-preview attendance
- **Origin:** Mendez's South Cone consultancy cover.
- **Use:** BA, occasional Geneva.
- **Strength:** social and visual; legitimate reason to be in proximity to targets.
- **Limit:** small world; faces are remembered.

### Customs-clearance pretext
- **Origin:** Meridian regional desk.
- **Use:** Karachi (KESTREL cultivation).
- **Strength:** justifies regular contact with freight operators.
- **Limit:** subject to local regulatory observation.

## Discipline

CFA must be capable of withstanding a 30-minute friendly interrogation by a competent civilian. If not, it is not a cover; it is a cosmetic.
