> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Principles

The Bureau does not run SIGINT collection. The Bureau **defends against** SIGINT collection by hostile services. Hardening is a posture, not a project.

## Officer-level hardening

1. Personal device discipline: no operational identifiers, no operational contacts on civilian phones.
2. Burner discipline: see [burner-rotation](burner-rotation.md).
3. Voice comms: face-to-face by default. App-based only when face-to-face is infeasible.
4. Metadata hygiene: do not establish patterns. Vary times, vary routes, vary devices.

## Asset-level guidance

Assets receive a tailored brief; the canonical structure is:
- What to never put in writing.
- What to never carry on their person.
- When to use the distress code-phrase.

## Adversary capability assessment

THORNHILL is assessed as **moderate** SIGINT-capable; OBSIDIAN GROUP as **opportunistic** rather than systemic.
