> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Policy

Each officer-asset pair shall use a dedicated burner pair, rotated on a 30-day cadence as standing default. High-tempo desks (Berlin Q2 2026) rotate at 14 days.

## Procurement

- Acquired through commercial channels, paid in cash.
- Numbers never associated with Bureau identifiers.
- Devices destroyed at rotation, not resold.

## Activation

- Burners go live at end of life-cycle, not start. Standing burners are off and dark until rotation-into-service.
- Activation code-phrase delivered out-of-band at pre-rotation meet.

## Code-phrases

Code-phrases for distress, stand-down, and "all clear" are agreed at recruitment and refreshed annually.

- BLUEBIRD distress: "the train is late." (See [stand-down report](../../field-reports/berlin/2026-06-02-bluebird-stand-down.md).)
- Standard "all clear": asset-dependent.
