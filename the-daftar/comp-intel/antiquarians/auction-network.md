> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Topology

- **London hub:** [Halloran, E.](../../dossiers/poi/halloran-ezra.md) — Mayfair premises; principal node for manuscripts and works on paper.
- **Geneva house:** Mid-tier auction house, with junior cataloguer notionally [ASSET OWLET](../../dossiers/assets/owlet.md). Consignment intake; valuation; lot-curation.
- **Buenos Aires periphery:** [ASSET CARDINAL](../../dossiers/assets/cardinal.md) gallery and adjacent auction-preview circuit.
- **Cairo periphery:** intermittent presence (per Op [MARIGOLD](../../ops-closed/marigold/after-action.md)).
- **Karachi:** no documented Antiquarians footprint; possible indirect linkage via Razzaq corridor.

## Pattern of trade

- London-Geneva axis is the spine.
- Provenance freshening typically occurs at Geneva intake (see [provenance trail](../../field-reports/buenos-aires/2026-04-25-cardinal-provenance.md)).
- Auction outputs surface in London (high-value) or Geneva (mid-value).

## Open questions

- Does the Geneva house knowingly participate in the laundering, or is it a credulous intermediary?
- Is there a New York or Tokyo node we have not mapped?
