## Premise

Voss has been in post since at least 2022 and is older than the typical THORNHILL director profile suggests. Plan for the succession.

## Scenarios

### Scenario A — Continuity successor
The deputy steps up; Voss's stated position on contractors holds; the OBSIDIAN relationship (if real) remains routed via Frey-channel cut-outs. **High likelihood.** Minimal posture change.

### Scenario B — Reform successor
A reformist director repudiates contractor reliance and absorbs functions back into THORNHILL proper. **Medium likelihood.** Disruption to OBSIDIAN client mix; brief instability window we could exploit.

### Scenario C — Hardline successor
A successor doubles down on contractor model; OBSIDIAN relationship deepens and becomes overt. **Low likelihood**, but if it occurs the Bureau's posture should shift toward direct cross-service liaison rather than penetration.

### Scenario D — Political dismissal
Voss is removed by political signal, not retired. Successor's posture is unpredictable. **Low likelihood**, but the indicators (parliamentary heat) are visible.

## Indicators to watch

- Avarian parliamentary committee testimony cadence.
- Voss's foreign-travel pattern (a sharp drop precedes most directorial departures).
- Promotion announcements at the deputy level.
