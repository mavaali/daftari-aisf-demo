> **FICTIONAL · DEMO CORPUS — Bureau for Atypical Inquiry**

## Mandate

Op DOLPHIN-WAKE is the Bureau's primary FININT operation against the OBSIDIAN GROUP's payment corridor. Mandate granted 15 Jan 2026.

## Objective

Map the Karachi → Dubai → Geneva corridor used by OBSIDIAN GROUP to receive client retainers, with the specific goal of:

1. Identifying OBSIDIAN's principal clients via payment-pattern correlation.
2. Establishing the relationship between the Antiquarians' Geneva infrastructure and the OBSIDIAN settlement layer.
3. Producing actionable FININT for Estimates and (selectively) for cross-service liaison.

- Cover-for-action: Meridian commercial-clearance consultancy.

- Passive observation in port environments.
- No SIGINT.
- No kinetic action.
