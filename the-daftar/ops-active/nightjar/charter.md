## Mandate

Op NIGHTJAR is the Bureau's principal collection operation against the THORNHILL Berlin rezidentura. Mandate granted by the Registrar 01 Dec 2025; reviewed quarterly.

## Objective

Penetrate the THORNHILL Berlin station via clerk-level HUMINT (initially [ASSET BLUEBIRD]; second asset cultivation authorised once BLUEBIRD reaches steady state) to:

1. Confirm or refute the working order-of-battle.
2. Provide budget and personnel-trajectory product.
3. Establish, by indirect indicators, the relationship between Berlin rezidentura and Avarian state actors (Frey/MoD channel).
